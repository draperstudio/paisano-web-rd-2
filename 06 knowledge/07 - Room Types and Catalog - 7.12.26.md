## WHAT THE PMS CAN AND CANNOT SHOW (WEB IMPLICATION)

- StayNTouch applies rate, max occupancy, and guest-facing description at the **room-type level only**. Features shown to guests must be consistent across every room in the type; per-room features (the wonky dial, the bigger living area) are internal-only. [SNT2]
- The fix on the PMS side is splitting types by delineating features that create jumps in value, for example "junior suite" and "junior suite with patio." Patio and balcony were named as the delineators. [SNT2]
- Buck, on the call: the team needs "that mirror database of information... so that we can serve that information up at least on the front end of our website." The website is where room-level detail lives, because the booking flow cannot carry it. [SNT2]
- Guest-facing descriptions in StayNTouch were not yet written or checked as of the catalog snapshot; BAR rates not yet set. [RC]
