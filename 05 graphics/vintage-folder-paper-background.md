# Vintage Folder Paper Background

**Code:** HP-GR-007

**Section:** graphics

**Type:** Background

A heavier aged paper, folder proportioned, with more visible grain and tone than the base paper. Reserved for feature and archival moments.

## Usage

**When to Use**
- On covers, section openers, and archival or history pages that want an older, tactile feel

**Do Not**
- Use it as the everyday ground; the base paper is the default
- Stretch it out of its proportion

## Specifications

Texture opacity: About 32 percent
Coverage: Full-bleed, 1920 by 1396, folder proportion
Role: Feature and archival surfaces, not the default
File: SVG with embedded raster
