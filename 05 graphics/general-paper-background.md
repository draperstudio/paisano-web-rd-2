# General Paper Background

**Code:** HP-GR-006

**Section:** graphics

**Type:** Background

The base paper grain. A large paper texture laid full-bleed under light layouts at very low opacity, so a page reads as printed on warm stock rather than flat white. This is the default surface for the brand.

## Usage

**When to Use**
- Under every light-ground layout, as the default surface behind content

**Do Not**
- Raise the opacity until the grain competes with the type
- Place it on the oxblood ground, which carries no paper texture

## Specifications

Texture opacity: About 12 percent
Blend: Plus-darker, darkening the ground slightly
Coverage: Full-bleed, 1920 by 1920 tile
Ground: Warm gesso cream
File: SVG with embedded raster
