# Facade Illustration

**Code:** HP-GR-005

**Section:** graphics

**Type:** Illustration

A fine-line drawing of the 1930 Trost building: the Spanish Colonial Revival facade, cypress trees, terracotta roof, arched loggia, and the neon "The Hotel Paisano" script. It is the only illustrative graphic in the system.

## Usage

**When to Use**
- As a hero or feature graphic on covers, section openers, and history pages
- One per surface, with room around it

**Do Not**
- Shrink it into an icon or set it behind text
- Recolor it into full color
- Pair it with the crest on the same surface

## Specifications

Line color: Oxblood #42101B or ink #01090F on the paper ground
Placement: Centerpiece, with full breathing room
Scale: Large, where the line detail holds
File: SVG
