# Horizontal Rule

**Code:** HP-GR-003

**Section:** graphics

**Type:** Rule

A full-width hairline in near-black ink with a small diamond terminal at each end. It is the primary divider in the system.

## Usage

**When to Use**
- As the header rule across the top of a page
- As a section break or a line under a label
- As the footer line

**Do Not**
- Thicken it, or make it dashed or dotted
- Crop off the diamond terminals
- Stack more than one

## Specifications

Color: Ink #01090F
Weight: Hairline
Terminals: Diamond at both ends, kept intact
Width: Full column or full page
File: SVG
