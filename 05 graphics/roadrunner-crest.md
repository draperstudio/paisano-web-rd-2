# Roadrunner Crest

**Code:** HP-GR-001

**Section:** graphics

**Type:** Ornament

The paisano, or roadrunner, drawn as a fine-line circular medallion in oxblood. It is the signature brand stamp and the bird the hotel is named for.

## Usage

**When to Use**
- As a small footer stamp in the bottom-left corner of a page or slide, set next to a horizontal rule
- As a closing mark on a final or thank-you page
- On its own as a seal on covers and on dark oxblood grounds, reversed to the paper cream

**Do Not**
- Enlarge it into a hero graphic or place it in the middle of content
- Recolor it outside the two brand inks or add shadows and effects
- Crowd it against type or against the edge of the page

## Specifications

Color on light grounds: Oxblood #42101B
Color on dark grounds: Paper cream, reversed
Placement: Bottom-left footer, beside a horizontal rule
Scale: Small stamp, not a feature graphic
Clear space: At least the height of the bird on all sides
File: SVG
