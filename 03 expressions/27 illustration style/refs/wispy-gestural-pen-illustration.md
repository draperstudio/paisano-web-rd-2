# Wispy Gestural Pen Illustration

## Description

The illustration utilizes a wispy, gestural line quality that prioritizes movement over anatomical completion, featuring broken strokes and varying line weights. The drawing avoids uniform thickness, instead using quick, tapered marks that suggest form through a series of disconnected, sketchy pen-like paths. This technique creates a sense of lightness and spontaneity, where the eye connects the gaps between the fragmented, expressive outlines.
