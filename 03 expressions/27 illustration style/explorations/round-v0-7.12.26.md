## The recipe (what produced these)

Prompt contract used for all three — this is the reusable part if any
candidate holds:

- subject drawn "in a single thin line" in a brand color on a flat solid ground
- "loose and gestural like a quick ink sketch: broken contours with visible
  gaps and disconnection between strokes, lines that lift off and don't close
  shapes, plenty of air and empty space inside forms"
- hard negatives: NO hatching, NO shading, NO fill, no texture on the ground
- "abstract and boiled down: suggest rather than render faithfully"
- generous margins, centered

  Whether that survives Buck's eye is exactly what this round tests.
