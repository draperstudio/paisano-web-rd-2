# Round two — v0, 7.12.26

Nothing removed. Vars 1–4 stand as round one.

## What re-studying the ref exposed

The ref is a color-field poster and round one got its weight distribution
backwards. In the ref: the ground is the brand color edge to edge; the
title is enormous (two stacked lines eating the top third, both lines
optically equal width); the photo is a small jewel about a third of the
frame wide, held in a tone-on-tone embossed frame; below it, a bold
typewriter-caps line pair, then a small justified paragraph. Round one
put a small title over a large framed photo on a pale ground.
