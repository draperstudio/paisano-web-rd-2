# Split-Frame Carousel with Left-Heavy Composition

## Description

This layout utilizes a distinct split-frame carousel structure where the left panel features a deeply recessed photographic inset with a thick cream border, creating a 'window' effect. While the left side is visually dense with interior photography, the right panel currently functions as a minimalist canvas for typography, requiring additional graphic layers or subtle textures to balance the visual weight of the primary image. The interaction between the framed photography and the open space on the right suggests a narrative flow that needs more supporting content in the secondary frame.
