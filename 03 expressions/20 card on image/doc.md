## What it is

A card containing an image on one side and text on the other, placed over a full background image. Round-one exploration with no reference behind it. The structure is liked; nearly every execution detail in the current exploration is off.

## What Buck likes

"I do like this card thing on an image... I like the image-left text thing right or vice versa in a card on top of a background image." Even the tolerated full bleed: "I don't really love the full bleed of the image but actually it's better here than it is in other places."

## Control for (locked)

- **Imagery (containment):** background image as ground + a framed card floating on it — the two-layer structure is the expression.
- **Zones (inside the card):** image one side, text the other — either handedness.

## Leave open (variation space)

- Image selection — "totally, totally, totally, totally fucking off" in the exploration; media controls fill at production.
- Type — "the type selection, the case, the kerning, the sizing is totally fucking off and needs to be fucking dialed" (brand-wide question).
- Eyebrow placement — the off-to-the-left eyebrow read as weird; where the eyebrow sits is open.
- Whether the background bleeds or gets the brand's held containment — full bleed is "better here than other places" but not loved; cycle both.

## Negative
