# High-Contrast Serif Typographic Framing

## Description

This layout utilizes a rigid typographic frame where all-caps serif text is pinned to the extreme top and bottom margins to create a structural container for the content. The design relies on extreme letter-spacing in the serif headers to span the full width of the frame, contrasted against centered italicized title-case subtext and justified body copy. Over the imagery, the wide-tracked all-caps serif is used as a transparent overlay, maintaining the same spatial rhythm and alignment as the white-space sections.
