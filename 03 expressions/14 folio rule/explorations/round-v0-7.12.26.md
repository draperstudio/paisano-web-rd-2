## What was tried

Built as a reusable `FolioRule` component — rule above, one/two lines of
microtext, "/NN" sub-number at the right baseline — deployed at the foot
of three real sections (courtyard, sign, arcade plates) so it's judged
in place:

- **var-1** — serif italic lines, the ref's short stub rule. Warmest.
- **var-2** — typewriter lines: the same voice as 11's metadata layer,
  testing whether the folio belongs to the microtext family's system.
- **var-3** — full-width hairline instead of the stub: the folio as a
  section boundary rather than a gesture.

The sub-numbers are real (courtyard /02, sign /04, arcade /05 — the
field-guide plate order from the 09 round), not decoration.
