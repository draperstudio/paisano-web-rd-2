## What re-studying the ref exposed

Round one extracted the folio moment (rule + lines + /05) and mounted
it under a big centered image — but the ref is a whole CARD with a
split-field anatomy: the photo owns the top ~55% full bleed to the
card edges; the lower field carries a staggered two-block statement in
tracked caps (first block set left, second block set right), then the
short stub rule alone on the left, then the two serif lines with the
/05 folio at bottom right. The stagger and the field split are what
give the foot its authority.
