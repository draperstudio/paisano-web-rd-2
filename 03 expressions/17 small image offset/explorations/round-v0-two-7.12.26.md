## What re-studying the ref exposed

The ref's power is the EXTREMITY of its three moves, and round one
undersold all of them: (1) the large image runs nearly the full frame
height, not two-thirds; (2) the small image is a genuinely tiny jewel
— isolated high and right, with real void between it and everything
else; (3) the text block sits LOW, far below the jewel, with a deep
first-line indent (~3.5em) that round one skipped entirely.
