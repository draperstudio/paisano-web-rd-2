The systematic typographic grid layout — content organized under Roman-numeral ordering, with offset micro text working inside a disciplined grid. Round one produced quite a few explorations; they progressively drifted from the references, and the references are the standard.

## What Buck likes

The farther right you go, the less I like them and to me they don't look like the references.
