# 07 The Restoration, As It Happens

**Goal** — Turn the renovation from a liability (unfinished rooms) into the brand's running story, and make the public timeline the proof of seriousness.

**Move** — Skeptics and repeat visitors from doubt to trust. A stated schedule is a commitment mechanism: publishing dates and then hitting them builds the trust the rooms cannot yet.

**Person & want** — Comparison-stage planners, plus the returning visitor who knew the old Paisano. Fear & Risk plane: this is lipstick on the old hotel. Live want: security, and curiosity (people follow a good in-progress story).

**Message** — What is done, what is under way, what is next, with dates. Now: restaurant, lobby, and courtyard in Phase I. Fall 2026: Meseta targeted. January 2027: rooms begin. Nothing framed as open that is not open.

**Angle** — Process. The work itself is the content: a room coming back, the kitchen taking shape, the trades. On the site it is a timeline; on social it becomes the room-by-room series the strategy names as an opportunity.

**Execution** — A timeline structure with few words per row and hard dates. Renders labeled as renderings. This section absorbs all forward-looking claims so the rest of the site never has to hedge.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 06 dossier (phasing, status, dates). Media: render-courtyard, render-exterior, render-bathroom.

**Board** — Band 16 (in-line section breaks, data table) fits the timeline treatment.
