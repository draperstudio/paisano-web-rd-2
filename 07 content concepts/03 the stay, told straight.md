# 03 The Stay, Told Straight

**Goal** — Get the room question answered honestly enough that a booking happens now, at today's product, without poisoning trust for the renovated product later.

**Move** — Trip-planners in comparison mode (vs the St. George, a rental, the old Paisano) from evaluating to trusting. The strategy says room and bathroom photos decide this; right now we cannot fully lead with rooms, so candor does the work photography will do later.

**Person & want** — All three segments at the comparison stage. Practical plane: a comfortable, quiet bed after a long drive. Live want: security. Fear: overpaying for the unrenovated thing.

**Message** — Simple rooms, honest rates, and a full room renovation beginning January 2027. What you get today is stated plainly, and what is coming is dated. Count honesty per 06 knowledge/07: today is 42 rooms across nine classes (Mezzanine Patio, Queen Historic, 2 Queen Historic, Deluxe Historic, Junior Suite, Courtyard Patio, 1BR Suite, 2BR Suite, and the Rock Hudson Suite); "48 rooms across six types" is the planned post-renovation state and gets framed as coming, never as current.

**Angle** — Catalog with candor. Rooms by type as they actually are, renders labeled as renderings, no seduction.

**Execution** — The rooms section: current room photography where it holds up, render-bedroom and render-bathroom clearly labeled as the January 2027 product, rate honesty, dogs and one-night stays stated here where the decision happens. One structural fact makes this section load-bearing, not decorative: the booking engine shows descriptions at room-type level only, so the site is the only place room-level detail can live (Buck on the SNT call: "we need that mirror database of information... so we can serve that information up at least on the front end of our website"). Two honesty guards from the catalog: don't market Juliet balconies (the renovation removes them), and don't promise the 2BR Suite forward (its class dies in the renovation).

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 06 knowledge/07 (room types, classes, callouts, open items), 06 dossier (renovation phasing, rates), RN walkthrough facts. Media: lobby-seating, render-bedroom, render-bathroom.

**Board** — Band 6 (stacked card layout) and band 5 (split-frame carousel) are candidate carriers for room types.
