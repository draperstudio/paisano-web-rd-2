# 05 Fed Seven Days

**Goal** — Kill the classic Marfa trip-killer: the fear that nothing is open on a Tuesday. The strategy names this as one of the practical questions that decides whether a trip happens this month or gets postponed.

**Move** — Practical planners from postponing to booking a date. This is a timing move, not an awareness move.

**Person & want** — Road-trippers arriving midweek and art-trip couples planning around the calendar. Practical plane: will we actually be fed. Live want: security. Loss framing is live here: book the wrong town on the wrong night and you eat gas-station food.

**Message** — Food and drink on site, seven days a week: dinner at Meseta, the cafe for breakfast and lunch, the bar at night. Framed exactly as far along as reality: Meseta is targeted for fall 2026 with Brian Espinosa as incoming chef, and what is on site meanwhile is stated plainly.

**Angle** — Guide first, with a profile thread (Espinosa and the ranches behind the menu) that spends later as its own piece. One subject, several depths.

**Execution** — The food section: the seven-day promise as the headline fact, the schedule as structure, Meseta as the coming thing clearly dated, desert-food adjacency (Chihuahuan ranching) as flavor in captions rather than a lecture.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 04 desert/ranching/food doc, 06 dossier (F&B phasing, Meseta timeline). Media: lobby-lounge-arch, lobby-lounge-doors, render-courtyard.

**Board** — Band 16 (menu layout) touches this directly.
