# 14 The Art Calendar

**Goal** — Give repeat visitors a reason to come back to the site between trips, and give trips a date. The strategy names the calendar angle and lists the art calendar as an opportunity: Chinati, Ballroom Marfa, and the hotel gallery.

**Move** — People who follow Marfa from afar to picking a date. A trip with no date is the enemy; what's-on is how dates happen.

**Person & want** — Awareness and consideration art-trip people. Experiential plane: be there when something is happening. Live want: curiosity, belonging.

**Message** — What is on in Marfa and at the hotel, and when: openings, installations, the hotel gallery, the town's seasonal rhythm.

**Angle** — Calendar. Dated entries, kept current, honest about the volatility (things change; each entry carries when it was checked). Hard rule: list, don't interpret. The Marfa art crowd is hostile to institutions borrowing Judd's weight, so the hotel lists what is on and does not explain the art or editorialize Chinati. Names, dates, and hours.

**Execution** — A living what's-on section, lighter than the evergreen guide (08): the guide answers how to do Marfa, this answers when. The hotel gallery earns its brand presence here as a participant in the calendar, not an ad.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 02 Marfa doc (institutions, calendar patterns, volatility warning). Media: facade-entrance, lobby shots.

**Board** — Uses the guide and micro-copy expressions; no dedicated band.
