# 11 Gatherings

**Goal** — Open the events pipeline: weddings, dinners, buyouts, and room blocks are the highest-value bookings the property takes, and their planners start months out.

**Move** — Event planners and engaged couples from evaluating the property to inquiring. The move completes when a real person answers quickly, so the content's job is to make inquiring feel safe and immediate.

**Person & want** — The planner segment from the brief: their questions are logistical (capacity, ceremony spot, dinner, block, one property or several). Social and emotional planes both live: this is the most important party they will ever throw, staged somewhere with a story.

**Message** — Courtyard ceremonies, indoor events, room blocks, and full-hotel buyouts, all on one property at the center of town, and a named contact who answers.

**Angle** — Demonstration. Show the courtyard and the spaces doing what they do; state capacities and the inquiry path plainly. The building and history concepts do the romancing; this one does the logistics.

**Execution** — The gatherings section: the courtyard as the lead image, the spaces listed with real capacities, dates and blocks holdable in advance, inquiry with a real contact rather than a form into the void.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 06 dossier (event spaces, capacities, operations). Media: courtyard-fountain, render-courtyard, lobby-lounge-arch.

**Board** — No dedicated band; leans on the same expressions as the stay and building sections.
