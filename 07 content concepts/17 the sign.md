# 17 The Sign

**Goal** — Give the brand's most recognizable image its own story. The strategy says discovery is mostly visual and names the neon as the most recognizable image; a single-subject page turns the thing people already save on Instagram into a front door.

**Move** — Savers and sharers from recognizing the image to knowing whose it is. The photo already circulates constantly; this page is where it points.

**Person & want** — Awareness-stage people who follow design, type, and neon; the same crowd the script expressions court. Live want: curiosity, identity.

**Message** — The rooftop neon has been read from Highway 90 since 1930, it is not changing, and here is its story: the script, the light, and the repairs over the years.

**Angle** — Scene crossed with archive. Mostly photography, night-heavy; the facts arrive in captions.

**Execution** — A short standalone page: the sign at night, close and far, archival beside current. Also the honest home of the script-typeface lineage, which matters because the whole brand type question orbits this sign. One story told well, spent once.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 01 building doc (the neon, the script). Media: neon-sign-day, neon-sign-closeup.

**Board** — Bands 1, 10, 26, 29 (the script/preloader material) all trace back to this subject; the open logo-script decision lives next to it and stays open.
