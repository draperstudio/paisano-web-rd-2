# 08 The Best Seat In Town

**Goal** — Make the Paisano the base of every Marfa trip plan. The strategy is explicit: content that helps someone plan a Marfa trip serves the hotel even when the hotel is not the subject, and the Paisano can cover the town credibly because it is the building at its center.

**Move** — Trip-planners from comparing hotels to planning their trip from the Paisano outward. Endowment is the mechanism: once their itinerary is organized around the hotel, the booking is defending something that already feels theirs.

**Person & want** — Art-trip couples (Chinati, Ballroom, the calendar) and road-trippers (what is open midweek). Practical plane: real answers. Experiential plane: a trip that goes right.

**Message** — Everything worth doing in Marfa, answered straight from the center of town: when to come, what is actually open on which days, how to do Chinati Weekend, where the Lights are.

**Angle** — Guide. Names, hours, days, and distances, with the volatility handled honestly (Marfa businesses change constantly; the guide says what was true and when it was checked).

**Execution** — The Marfa section on the site, pointing to a fuller guide. One detail worth owning: the hotel and the Marfa Lights share a name, Paisano Pass, where the first sighting was reported in 1883. Nine miles east, viewing center open all night. That connection is ours to tell.

**Draws on** — Knowledge: 02 Marfa town/art/culture doc, 03 region doc. Media: marfa-street-sunset, facade-entrance.

**Board** — Band 15 (botanical editorial, parenthetical numbering) and the micro-copy expression fit guide entries.
