## Description

This wide shot architectural rendering showcases the new post-renovation courtyard entry wall and gates of The Hotel Paisano under a clear, bright sky. The facade features white stucco walls adorned with lush green climbing vines and elegant arched wrought-iron gates that lead into a central courtyard. Prominent red neon signage displaying the hotel's name is positioned both above the central entryway and atop the right wing of the building, creating a striking contrast against the neutral tones. Tall, slender cypress trees and Mediterranean-style architectural details, such as the decorative parapet and terracotta roof tiles, emphasize a sophisticated and historic aesthetic.
