# lobby-lounge-arch.jpg

## Description

This wide shot captures a sitting area in the lobby near the lounge, showcasing a rich blend of Southwestern and Spanish Colonial architectural styles. The space features vintage leather armchairs arranged around a cowhide rug, set against a backdrop of intricate geometric tile wainscoting and textured plaster walls. Natural light streams through a large window on the left, illuminating the warm earth tones of the hexagonal floor tiles and the large arched entryway that leads deeper into the hotel. Decorative elements like a framed historical photograph, turned wood pillars, and classic lamps enhance the sophisticated, historic atmosphere of the property.
