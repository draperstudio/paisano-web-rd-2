# marfa-street-sunset.jpg

## Description

This photo captures Main Street in Marfa, Texas, looking directly toward the historic Presidio County Courthouse under a dramatic sunset sky. The Hotel Paisano is situated on the left, framed by tall, slender cypress trees and parked vehicles along the curb. The composition uses the yellow double lines of the asphalt road as leading lines to draw the viewer's eye toward the domed courthouse in the distance. Warm, golden light from the setting sun illuminates the voluminous clouds, creating a serene and expansive atmosphere characteristic of West Texas. This asset highlights the unique architectural charm and quiet, open-road aesthetic of a iconic desert destination.
