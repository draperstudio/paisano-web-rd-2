# archival-facade-1940s.png

## Description

This black and white archival photograph captures the historic Hotel Paisano in Marfa, Texas, showcasing its Spanish Colonial Revival architecture. A massive neon sign reading 'HOTEL PAISANO' is mounted on a steel scaffold atop the roof, serving as a prominent landmark against a cloudy sky. Several vintage 1940s-era automobiles are parked along the street in front of the building's storefronts and ornate arched entrance. The composition emphasizes the building's corner location, highlighting the textured stucco walls, decorative stonework, and striped awnings that define its classic aesthetic.
