# lobby-lounge-doors.jpg

## Description

This narrow vertical shot captures a cozy sitting area in a hotel lobby near the lounge, framed by a large textured archway. The foreground features vintage leather armchairs with wooden spindles and a matching ottoman set upon a cowhide rug and intricate geometric floor tiles. In the background, a second lounge area is visible through dark wooden pillars, featuring a fireplace, warm lamps, and glass-paned double doors that let in soft natural light. The color palette is dominated by warm earth tones, including terracotta, cream, and deep browns, creating an inviting and historic atmosphere.
